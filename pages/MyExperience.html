My Experience

UCSB Health & Wellness -- Data Management Intern

From September 2024 to June 2025, I worked as a Data Mamagement Intern for UCSB Health & Wellness, where I supported campus-wide public health initiatives through data analysis, automation, and visualization. In this role, I built interactive Tableau dashboards to generate self-updating reports on Health & Wellness event metrics, reaching over 1,000 stakeholders including departments and donors. I also conducted longitudinal analysis of two decades of UCSB alcohol consumption survey data— representing over 20,000 students—to guide a social norms campaign. To streamline data workflows, I developed Python automation scripts that extracted and organized Qualtrics survey data for the UCSB Sleep Challenge, reducing processing time by 70%. Additionally, I maintained and updated the UCSB Health & Wellness website using Drupal CMS and HTML, ensuring timely delivery of public health messaging and resources.

This experience strengthened my ability to translate complex datasets into actionable insights, improved my technical fluency across data tools, and deepened my commitment to using data for meaningful, real-world impact.

UCSB Housing -- Resident Assistant

I became a Resident Assistant (RA) as I entered my third year of college, and it has been one of the most rewarding experiences of my undergraduate journey. In this role, I have grown both as a leader and as a community builder, supporting residents through academic and personal challenges while fostering an inclusive living environment. As the only international student on my team, I brought unique perspectives that enriched discussions and decision-making, ensuring that diverse voices were considered in our programming.

Now, as a returner RA, I take on a leadership role, guiding a team of 17 RAs by sharing my experiences, answering questions, and assisting in daily tasks, event planning, and community development. This experience has strengthened my problem-solving, teamwork, and leadership skills, preparing me to navigate complex challenges in both professional and academic settings.

UCSB Instructional Development -- Student Staff

Additionally, I conducted regular inspections to ensure all equipment was in optimal condition and properly set up for lectures and events.

This experience strengthened my problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and ability to deliver efficient technical support in a fast-paced academic environment.

UCSB Louis Sensory Systems and Behavior Lab -- Research Assistant

As a Research Assistant at the UCSB Louis Sensory Systems and Behavior Lab, I focus on developing and refining data analysis pipelines for larval movement tracking. Using PiVR, I extract precise movement data—head, midpoint, and tail positions—to support machine learning applications in behavioral analysis. I have fine-tuned classification models, improving prediction accuracy from 68% to 74% through hyperparameter optimization and data preprocessing. Beyond technical development, I collaborate closely with lab members to enhance model performance and contribute to a deeper understanding of larval movement patterns.

This experience has strengthened my skills in data analysis, machine learning, and interdisciplinary research, furthering my passion for applying computational techniques to biological studies.
